We are interested in the studies of nuclear structure, single and double-beta decay, nucleosynthesis, hypernuclei, equation of state of nuclear matter, neutron stars, BSM physics with ab initio many-body methods, (lattice) chiral EFT, density functional theory, and beyond mean-field approaches, etc. and trying to address the following questions:
  • Where do nuclei and elements come from?
  • What combinations of neutrons and protons can form a bound atomic nucleus?
  • How does QCD in the low-energy regime manifests itself in nuclear phenomena?
  • How does a highly organized complex system exhibit regular patterns and symmetries?
  • What's the equation of state of superdense nuclear matter?
  • Can we test fundamental symmetries in rare isotopes?
